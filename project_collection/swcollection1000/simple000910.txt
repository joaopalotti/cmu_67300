 Tottenham Hotspur Football Club  is an  England English   Association football football  club. The club plays in the  English Premier League Premier League . Their current  stadium  is  White Hart Lane , in north  London . The captain of Tottenham Hotspur is Hugo Lloris most of the time.  Mauricio Pochettino  is the manager.  The club's most common nickname is Spurs. Other nicknames include The Lilywhites and occasionally "Yid Army".

Famous Tottenham Hotspur players include  Pat Jennings ,  Paul Gascoigne ,  Glenn Hoddle ,  Dave Mackay ,  Gary Lineker ,  Danny Blanchflower ,  Teddy Sheringham ,  Josh Kennard and  Jimmy Greaves . They have many rivals in London, but their most famous rivals are  Arsenal F.C. Arsenal . Tottenham were the first winners of the UEFA Cup tournament, and the first team in the 20th century to achieve the league and cup double. In the  2015-16 Premier League , they finished in 3rd position and earned a place in the  European Champions League .
