 Herm  is the  small est of the  Channel Islands  that is open to the public.

Herm is only 11/2 miles long.  Car s are banned from the small  island  just like its Channel Island neighbour,  Sark . Unlike Sark,  bicycle s are banned too. The sandy white beaches make Herm a walker's paradise.
