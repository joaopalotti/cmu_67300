Gas syringe

Image:Gas syringe.jpg thumb|200px|A gas syringe showing its components separate and together 

A  gas syringe  is a  tool  used in a  laboratory . It is used to collect a  gas  or to  Measurement measure  the  volume  of a gas.

Simple systems can be settled in the laboratory to collect gas or to compress it.  It is usually used to explain Gas Law.
