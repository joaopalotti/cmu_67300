Bambuco

Bambuco  is a type of  music  from  Colombia . The  beat (music) beat  of this music is like the  Europe an  waltz (music) waltz  or  polska . Bambuco is often  folk music . There is a dance that goes with Bambuco music. It is a group dance and has a 6/8 or 3/4  metre (music) meter .

Sometimes Bambuco is called  "el bambuco" . People think Bambuco started in  Africa . It is popular in the Andean region of Colombia and throughout  Latin America .
