FAQ

FAQ  is an  abbreviation  for "Frequently Asked Question(s)". The term is used for a list  question s and  :wikt:answer answers . All of the questions are supposed to be asked often and they all are about the same thing. Since the acronym was first used in written form, there are different ways it is said; both "fak" and "F.A.Q." are commonly used.
