 Argon  is a  chemical element . The symbol for argon is  Ar , and its  atomic number  (or  proton  number) is 18. It is a  noble gas  and no  electron s or protons can be lost or gained from this atom.

Argon  atom s are found in  air . About 1% of the  Earth 's  atmosphere  (the air around us) is argon.
