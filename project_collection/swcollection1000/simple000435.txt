The  Saint Lawrence River  ( lang-fr fleuve Saint-Laurent ;  Tuscarora language Tuscarora :  Kahnawa'kye ; ref Rudes, B.  Tuscarora English Dictionary  Toronto: University of Toronto Press, 1999 ref   Mohawk language Mohawk :  Kaniatarowanenneh , meaning "big waterway") is a big  river  in eastern  North America . It flows between the  Canada Canadian   province  of  Ontario  and the  United States American   state  of  New York , and through the Canadian province of  Quebec . 

The river drains water from the  Great Lakes  into the  Atlantic Ocean . It is more than three thousand  kilometre s long. The river meets the Atlantic Ocean in a big " estuary " or bay, the biggest in the world; this is called the  Gulf of Saint Lawrence .

The Canadian cities of  Kingston ,  Montreal ,  Trois-Rivieres, Quebec Trois-Rivieres  and  Quebec City  are on this river. It is very important because  ship s can sail up the river and through the Great Lakes right into the middle of North America. It is the third largest river in Canada.
