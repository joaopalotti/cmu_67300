The  1960s  was the  decade  that started on January 1, 1960 and ended on December 31, 1969. Many things happened in the sixties, including the  Space Race , the  African-American Civil Rights Movement Civil Rights movement , and the  Vietnam War .

The 1960s term also refers to an  era  more often called  the Sixties . This was a set of  culture cultural  and  Politics political  trends around the globe. The social revolution of the 1960s was part of a wider  counterculture . Old ways were changed, new ways taken up. Typical was the introduction of the  birth control pill , and its effect on  Sexual intercourse sexual activity , widespread use of certain  Illegal drugs drugs  and a general disrespect for traditional ways.
