Homonym

A  homonym  is a  word  that has more than one meaning. For example,  right  means  moral , the opposite of  left , and a personal  freedom .

Definition: one of a group of words that share the same  orthography spelling  and  pronunciation  but have different meanings. ref name RHUD http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/homonym homonym ,  Random House unabridged dictionary  at dictionary.com ref 

The point is that homonyms are both  homophone s (they sound the same) and  homograph s (they look the same).
