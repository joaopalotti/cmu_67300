 Bolton Wanderers F.C.  is an  England English   Association football football  club. It is based 15  mile s north of  Manchester . The club plays in the Championship, the second highest league in England. Their home is the  Reebok Stadium . It holds around 28,723 people. Their manager is Dougie Freedman. At home they play in a  white  shirt, navy blue shorts & white socks. Away they play in a navy blue shirt, white shorts and navy blue socks.
