Modern cosmology is dominated by the  Big Bang  theory, which brings together observational astronomy and  particle physics . ref http://oxforddictionaries.com/definition/cosmology ref  

Though the word  cosmology  is recent (first used in 1730 in  Christian Wolff 's  Cosmologia Generalis ), the study of the universe has a long history.

 History 
Until recently, people thought that the  universe  was the  Milky Way . They thought this because they could only see the planets up to  Saturn (planet) Saturn  and  star s. With the invention of the  telescope , our universe got bigger. Even in the 20th century, people thought that the Milky Way was the universe. With the  Hubble Space Telescope , people could see things far away.
