Temple

File:Roma-tempio di antonio-faustina.jpg thumb|250px|Temple of Antoninus and Faustina,  Rome ,  Italy . 

A  temple  is a building where people go to practice their  religion . In a temple people may perform religious  ritual s,  ceremony ceremonies , and  pray . Thus, a temple is a general term for a house of  worship .  Christianity Christians  usually call their religious buildings  church churches .
