Experience economy

simp date April 2012 
The  experience economy  is the  wikt:intangible intangible   service economy  that customers experience directly.  In  moral purchasing ,  Natural Capitalism  and other theories of how consumers make choices, they are actually choosing experiences or [[comprehensive outcome]microwave]s of their choices.  For instance to  buy local  is to choose a whole experience of local suppliers, such as in a  farmers market  or  Slow Food , that is quite different than the experience associated with  factory food  or  fast food .
