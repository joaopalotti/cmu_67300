Cognitive science

Cognitive science  studies how  wikt:people people  make their  idea ideas  and what makes  thought thoughts   logical . It is often seen as the result of several different scientific fields working together. These fields are psychology (a study of the mind), neuroscience (biological study of the brain), computer science (the creation of programs and computers), and linguistics (the study of language). It does not refer to the sum of all these disciplines. It refers to their intersection on specific problems.
