The  Moselle River  ( lang-de Mosel )  is a river which flows through   France  and  Luxembourg , then becomes part of the  Rhine River  after it flows into  Germany . It is  convert 545 km mi  long.

Important cities at the Moselle river are  Metz ,  Thionville ,  Trier ,  Bernkastel-Kues  and  Koblenz .

The valley of the Moselle river is famous for the wine of  Elbling ,  Riesling , and  Muller-Thurgau  - grapes in the wine growing region  Moselle-Saar-Ruwer .
