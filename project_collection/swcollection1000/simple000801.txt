1920s

decadebox 192 

The  1920s  was the decade that started on January 1, 1920 and ended on December 31, 1929. This decade was known as the  Roaring Twenties  or the Jazz Age.

  The most important events  

   Golden Twenty 
   1920  -  Prohibition  began in the  United States .
   1922  -  Howard Carter  discovered the tomb of the  Egypt ian King  Tutankhamun .
   1922  -  Republic of Ireland Southern Ireland  gained independence from the  United Kingdom .
   1929  - The  Black Tuesday
