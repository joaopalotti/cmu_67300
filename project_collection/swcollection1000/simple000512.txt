Vegetable oil

Vegetable oils  are a kind of  oil  made from many different  plant s.  They are used in  food  and for  cook ing. Some kinds of plant oils that people use are  African oil palm ,  maize ,  Olive (fruit) olive ,  peanut ,  rapeseed ,  soy bean soy , and  sunflower .

 Margarine  is an  wikt:artificial artificial   butter  made from vegetable oil.
