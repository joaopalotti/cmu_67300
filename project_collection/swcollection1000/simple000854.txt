 Saarlouis  is a  city  in the  Saarland  ( Germany ). It has 38.400 inhabitants and is on the  Saar . From 1936 to 1945 it was called  Saarlautern .  Ford Motor Company  has a plant here; it produces the  Ford Focus Focus ,  Ford C-Max C-Max  and  Ford Kuga Kuga .
