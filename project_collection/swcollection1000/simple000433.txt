Sport

A  sport  is commonly defined as an athletic activity or skill and involves a degree of  competition , such as  baseball ,  soccer ,  bowling , or  basketball . Some  game s and many kinds of  racing  are called sports. Many people play sports with their friends.  Sportsperson They  need  Coach (sport) coaches  to teach or train  team s or individuals how to do better. Sports can be played  indoors  or  Position outdoors  and by individuals or teams.

Some people like to watch other people play sports. Those who watch others playing sports are called  fanatic fan s . While some fans watch sports on  television , others actually go to  stadium s or other places where people pay to watch them in person. These fans are called  spectators .
