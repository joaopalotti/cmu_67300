 Nauru  is a small  island  in the  Pacific Ocean . It's 42 kilometers south of the equator and has a surface area of 23.3km2. This one island is one country (a  republic ). It sells  phosphate  (a chemical) to  Australia . Nauru is the smallest republic in the world.  English language English  and  Nauruan language Nauruan  are the official languages of Nauru. Nauru has no official  Capital (city) capital , but its biggest city is  Yaren . The current president of Nauru is  Baron Waqa . There are 14  district s in Nauru:
