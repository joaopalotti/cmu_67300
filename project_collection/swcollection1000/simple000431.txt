Suggestion

A  suggestion  is an idea that one suggests, or says is  goodness good  for  another  (or  others ) to  follow . Some people may agree to it, and some may disagree. If they disagree or have a different suggestion, the person who first suggested the idea will usually discuss with the other people and find a good conclusion that satisfies both and is good.
