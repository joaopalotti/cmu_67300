Molokai

Molokai  (or  Moloka`i ) is the fifth largest  island  in the U.S.  Hawaiian Islands .  The island is 38  mile s long and 10 miles across. Its land area is 261 square miles.  The highest mountain is named  Kamakou , and it is 4,970  Foot_(unit_of_length) feet  (1,514  metre meters ) high. 

'Moloka'i is nicknamed "The Friendly Isle". It is one of the least developed of the Hawaiian islands.

The only big town is named  Kaunakakai, Hawaii Kaunakakai , which is also the main or  chief   port  on the island.  The  airport  is on West Moloka'i. Also on the island is   Kalaupapa , which is a place for people who have a diease called  leprosy . 

Moloka'i has many  Hawaiian fish pond s along its south  shore . Many of these have been cleaned and fixed.
