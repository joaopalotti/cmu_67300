A  dollar  is a type of  currency . Many  country countries  have named their money  the dollar , so it is important to say which dollar is being talked about. The symbol for the dollar is a capital letter S, pierced by one or two vertical lines:  $ .

The dollar is named after the  thaler . The thaler was a large silver coin first made in the year  1518 . The thaler named after the  Joachimsthal  (Joachim's valley) mine in  Bohemia  ( Thal  means  valley  in  German language German ). The later Spanish Peso was the same size and was often called " Spanish dollar ". In the 18th century it became a world currency. Many national currencies were originally Spanish dollars including the ones now called dollar or  peso  and the  Japanese yen ,  Indian rupee  and Chinese  Renminbi .
