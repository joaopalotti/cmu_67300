 Wigan Athletic Football Club  is an  England English   Association football football  club. The club plays in the  Football League Championship Championship , currently managed by  Owen Coyle . They play their home games at the  DW Stadium . In 2013 they won the  FA Cup , and so they played in the  UEFA Europa League  in the season 2013/14. A few days later they were relegated from the  English Premier League  to the  Football League Championship , becoming the first FA Cup-winning side to be relegated in the same season. Manager  Roberto Martinez  left the club shortly after to join  Everton F.C. Everton .  Owen Coyle  replaced him.
