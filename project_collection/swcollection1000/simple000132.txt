Embassy

Image:Riga DeutscheBotschaft.jpg thumb|250px|right| The  Germany German  embassy in  Riga 

A foreign  embassy  is the official office of one  country  in the  Capital (city) capital  of another country. It is where the  ambassador  (representative) of the home country works and it is where most of the  communication  (talk) between the two  governments  happen.
