LOL

LOL  is an  internet slang  term that means "laugh out loud". Sometimes it is used to mean "lots of love". 

It also means  mute  in  Persian language Persian  and is a native  Dutch language Dutch  word (not an acronym) which means "fun" (" wikt:lolling lolling " means "funny").
