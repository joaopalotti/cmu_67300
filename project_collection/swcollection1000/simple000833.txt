 Stuttgart  is a  city  in  Germany . It is the capital of the  States of Germany state of   Baden-Wurttemberg  and of the  Administrative District of Stuttgart  ( Regierungsbezirk ). Stuttgart is on the river  Neckar . In December 2011, 613,392 people live there. ref http://www.statistik.baden-wuerttemberg.de/Veroeffentl/Statistische_Berichte/3126_11001.pdf ref 

Stuttgart is currently when  the sixth largest German city and Stuttgart Region is the nation's third largest  region . The city is divided into 23 city  district s.

Stuttgart is the home of two  car  manufacturers:  Mercedes Benz  and  Porsche . Porsche is in Zuffenhausen (North) and Mercedes in Unterturkheim (Southeast).

Stuttgart has an  oceanic climate  ( Cfb  in the  Koeppen climate classification ).

  Population  
Stuttgart has 613,392 people within city limits. It is the 6th largest city in Germany after  Berlin ,  Hamburg ,  Munich ,  Cologne  and  Frankfurt am Main . According to a 2005 estimate, the Stuttgart urban area had between 1,238,000 and 1,250,000 people. Its  metropolitan area  has a  population  of 5.3 million (2008). ref cite web url http://www.deutsche-metropolregionen.org/mitglieder/stuttgart.html title de icon  Stuttgart work Initiativkreis Europaische Metropolregionen accessdate 23 March 2009 ref
