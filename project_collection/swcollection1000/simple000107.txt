Capitalization

Capitalise  ( Britain British  spelling) or  capitalize  ( North America n spelling) means to make one or more  letter s  Roman alphabet upper case .  The first letter of a  sentence  is capitalised in many  language s, as are the first letters of  proper noun s such as names of people and places. In  German language German , however, all nouns are capitalized. It is the only language to do that.

The  homonym   capitalize  is a different word, and means "to fully fund as an  investment ".
