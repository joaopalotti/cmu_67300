London Borough

A  London Borough  is a district of  London . There are 33 of them, including two that also call themselves  city cities  - the  City of Westminster  (where  Buckingham Palace  and the  Houses of Parliament  are) and the  City of London  (the oldest part of London). Each Borough has its own local  government .
