Height

Image:Height.svg thumb|right|Height 
 Height  is the  distance  between the lowest end and highest end of an  wikt:object object .

For example, people consider the bottom of the foot a person's lowest end, and the top of the head a person's highest end. If the distance between the bottom of a person's foot and the top of that person's head is 64  inch es, then that person's height is 64 inches.
