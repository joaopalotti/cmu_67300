Symbol

A  symbol  is a drawing, shape, or object that represents an idea, object, or amount of something.  

The most common symbols are  letter s, which are symbols of  word s and  sound s. A symbol can be an actual object (such as the cross, a symbol of  Christianity  or a scepter, a symbol of royalty and power), or a certain  color  or  pattern . Symbols are used often in  poetry  and other types of  literature , most often used as  metaphor s or  simile s.

A  national emblem  is a symbol for a certain country.
