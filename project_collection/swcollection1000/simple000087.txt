Chat

To  chat  is to  talk  about ordinary things that are not very important. A person can chat with another person, or to many people. People also use this word now for parts of the  Internet  where we can talk with many different people at the same time. Usually, people chat on the Internet in a  chat room  or  instant messaging messaging  service like  AOL AOL Instant Messenger  (AIM),  Yahoo! Yahoo Messenger   Windows Live Messenger  or  Tencent QQ . There are also programs which let people use different messaging services from one program, such as  Pidgin (software) Pidgin .
