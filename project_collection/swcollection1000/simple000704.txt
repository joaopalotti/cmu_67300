Alexander Gode

Alexander Gottfried Friedrich Gode-von-Aesch  or simply  Alexander Gode  ( October 30 ,  1906  -  August 10 ,  1970 ) a  Germany German - United States American   linguistics linguist  and  translation translator . He helped create  Interlingua .
